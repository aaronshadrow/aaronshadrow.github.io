Testimonials

I started taking lessons with Aaron 6 months ago to learn music, improve my guitar playing and write better songs after not making much progress teaching myself using Youtube.

Aaron explains music theory in a way that is easy to understand and tailored to the experience and knowledge of the student, providing you with visuals and useful tips for quick learning. He is a very supportive teacher, always pointing out your progress when you feel you haven’t made enough.

My favorite thing about our lessons, however, is how versatile they are. From learning different techniques to play electric guitar, acoustic guitar and piano to refining song writing. Aaron helped me with the structure, chord progression and playing techniques of my songs. They sound so much better now! I’ve been using what he taught me ever since. I highly recommend Aaron to students of all levels and musical interests!


Georgiana Sima

Aaron is hands down the best music teacher I’ve ever had, and I’ve had a lot of tuition over the years with various instruments and teachers. I’ve made more progress with Aaron in 9 months than I did with a previous teacher of three years. The lessons are really led by what you want to learn, but, as the expert, Aaron always has a suggestion for where to go next that will push you to the next level. His knowledge of music theory is astounding, but he makes it so accessible. I used to think I hated music theory but since learning with Aaron, I don’t feel that at all – now I’m watching YouTube videos on my own just because I’m interested!

The other invaluable side to Aaron’s teaching is his ability to build your confidence in yourself. I’ve struggled for over a decade insecurity paralysis – I’ve wanted to write music but my fear of it being cliché or just bad has always blocked me. Aaron, with his patience, encouragement and empathy, has managed coax my creativity out and I’ve actually started writing music that I want to play for others.
